Research: Human-like Autonomous Language Agents

I am devoted to making language agents have human-like cognitive abilities and aligning autonomous generative agents with human cognition, aiming to create agents that saying, thinking, and acting humanly with language:

  • Planning and Reasoning: Focus on equipping generative agents with human-level planning and reasoning capabilities through reflection, multi-agent collaboration, tool usage, and etc., as in Agent-R | EvoAgent | EasyTool | CoScript.
  • Cognitive Modeling: Explore cognitive knowledge (such as Analogy, Idiom, Pun and Role-playing) in generative agents to enable them to generate responses that align better with human cognition, as in SCUA | CROSS | InCharacter.
  • Knowledge Acquisition: Excavate cognitive knowledge based on generative agents to construct rich knowledge resources that can be effectively utilized, as in AnalogyKB | IdiomKB | KPCE.
